About Me

I am a front end developer with a passion for Javascript from Minneapolis, Minnesota.

I'm a very driven individual with a passion for learning. I know that there will always be things that I can improve about both myself and my coding, and I am always striving to be better than my best in both regards. I began learning code initially as a teenager as a hobby, and as an adult chose to study it as a trade. I especially love Javascript, and am currently very comfortable with jQuery. I want to continue learning different frameworks and libraries. I also have a passion for game development. However, career-wise, I have a passion for the outdoors, and I love to play video games in my spare time. My favorite currently running comic is Saga, and my most watched movie is Jurassic Park.
